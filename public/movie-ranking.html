The Best Movies According to Bianca

My top 3 movies of all-time

Magnifico - 2003 Film (Philippines)

Magnifico is a drama film which is mainly about a child who due to poverty, built a coffin for his terminally ill grandmother.This film has a great plot twist and is definitely a heart wrenching masterpiece.

Sky of Love - 2007 Film (Japan)

This film is all about a young couple's tragic love. It all went well in the beginning, but continuous misfortunes went their way which strengthened their love for each other. Definitely a tear jerking jewel.

Bliss - 2017 (Philippines)

Bliss is a psychological thriller film which is about an actress who had an accident during the filming of her new movie. A gem with an excellent plot twist that will actually twistyour hearts and minds.
